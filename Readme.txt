Installation Manual

WalletApp starts with a simple Home Screen with a Transfer button that helps in scanning devices nearby with enabled WiFi Direct service on their phones.

Steps to enable WiFi Direct:
1.Enable Wifi Connection.
2.Open Advanced settings in WiFi settings.
3.Select WiFi Direct Here.

Steps to proceed in app:
1.Once you have enabled WifiDirect,Click Transfer button.
2.User can enable Wifi Direct services from within the app from settings menu in toolbar.
3.List of all the nearby devices will be shown.
4.Name of Device and Device manufacturer has been shown for now.
